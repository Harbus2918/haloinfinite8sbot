Privacy Policy for Halo Infinite Game Series Bot

Last Updated: 10/08/2023

1. Introduction

Thank you for using the Halo Infinite Game Series Bot ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and aim to protect any information related to your interaction with our bot. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Data We Collect

The only data we collect is the number of times commands are called. We do not save any user-specific data or associate command usage with specific users.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data to:

  • Monitor and analyze the usage of commands for improvement purposes.
  • Again, no user data is kept or logged. Only which commands have been called.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained indefinitely and is not deleted unless done so manually for specific reasons.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or lease your data with third-party services or individuals.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Inquire about the data we collect.
  • Request no data be collected from you, though this may impact the bot's functionality for you.

7. Security

We implement robust security measures to protect the data we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will always display the most current version on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at idefend on Discord.
